Cannabis Pharmaceutical Growers Ltd is locally owned, and is operated by a friendly and extremely knowledgeable staff.

Cannabis Pharmaceutical Growers Ltd offers a variety of cannabis infused items including coffee, tea, smoothies, and more, as well as smokable vapes and flower.

Established in 2021 as a result of legalisation of Cannabis Pharmaceutical Growers Ltd production in Malawi , this family owned business was among the first group to obtain a grower's licence . Since then CPG has been thriving to be pioneers in the African cannabis industry in both quality and production volumes. Our inhouse expertise in the Pharmaceutical and Farming technology has proven to give us a competitive edge in this new and fast growing industry .
